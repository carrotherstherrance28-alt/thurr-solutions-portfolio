B2B Sales · Telecom · Revenue Operations

Telecom Lead Intelligence Engine

AI Scoring Email Validation Web Enrichment Slack Alerts
⚙ 29 nodes · n8n

Business Problem

SDRs spent 15 minutes per lead manually verifying emails, researching companies, scoring ICP fit, and updating spreadsheets. At 100 leads per month — 25 hours of SDR time wasted on data entry, costing $875/month with no consistency, no audit trail, and 15–20% invalid emails reaching outreach.

As-Is Process

SDRs manually verified emails, researched companies via LinkedIn and Google, scored ICP fit in spreadsheets, then updated tracking sheets and notified sales via ad-hoc Slack messages — creating inconsistent data quality and a 15-minute time sink per lead.

Automated Process

Daily Trigger→ ICP Filter→ Email Verify→ Web Research→ AI Score→ Slack Alert

Key Features

  • ICP pre-filter eliminates API cost for bad leads upfront
  • Bouncer email validation at 90+ confidence threshold
  • Tavily AI web research enriches every qualified lead
  • OpenRouter LLM scores leads 0–100 with written reasoning
  • Full audit trail written to Google Sheets automatically
  • Real-time monitoring dashboard with error logging

Client Onboarding Automation Engine

Welcome Automation Drive Folder Setup CRM Creation Slack Alerts
⚙ n8n · Google Drive · Airtable

Business Problem

After a roofing contract was signed, office staff manually emailed welcome packets, created Drive folders by hand, texted project managers, and logged client info into spreadsheets — taking 60–90 minutes per new client and creating frequent data entry errors across systems.

As-Is Process

Office staff manually emailed welcome packets, created Drive folders by hand, texted project managers, and logged client info into spreadsheets — creating frequent data entry errors and consuming 60–90 minutes per new client.

Automated Process

Contract Signed→ Welcome Email→ Drive Folders→ CRM Record→ PM Alert→ Follow-up Scheduled

Key Features
